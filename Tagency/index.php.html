T Agency is an Artist Management agency based out of Kolkata. T in the name stands for Talent and the idea is to recognize local talent and take it to the next level. T Agency also works closely with International Artists and provides promoters in India an opportunity to work with these Artists at affordable prices, making the audience's ticket even cheaper and work towards the larger goal of improving the Concert and EDM scene in India and taking it a notch higher.

T Agency is a project of Creative Cubes, a company formed in 2013 and has the accolades of managing the first ever SUNBURN Reload in West Bengal. Creative Cubes has worked with International Artists WolfPack, Marnik and Hard Candies and also Indian Artists Lost Stories.
